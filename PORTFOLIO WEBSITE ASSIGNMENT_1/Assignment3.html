Trip to Sydney

In June I went to sydney for a contemporary arts festival and the ensuing after party. When I travelled I stayed in a space themed pod hotel because it was the cheapest option available at the time. I arrived their 1 day early and spent my time exploring the city and going to recommended cafe's, bars and shops. the day after I attended Soft Centre and as it came to a close headed in an uber to the address I had been messaged from the organisers for the afterparty. I arrived in a daze and an old middle eastern man approached and whispered to me "this way" he lead me inside a regular seeming office building to the reception where someone was checking tickets. I walked upstairs to the designated floor. still with office equipment lying around. still in okay condition but with new lighting fitted and great speakers. I'm aiming to recrete these 3 spaces within my game. In a way that weekend almost felt like a game with the grungy futuristic aesthetic pervasive in all aspects of my journey.


3d Modelling, texturing and texture baking done in blender.
Below is my mockup in the cycles render engine for the abandoned office building

​

Using a blender plugin called scene city which had a tie in unity plugin I was able to easily customise a city area and export it into unity using their own script. I had plans to try to recreate an area in sydney but this was easier and less time consuming

For the Sleeping pod I kitbashed together some assets I found on Sketchfab and applied some emmision based light strips to the roof to give the blue glow ​

The office space once I had imported the models and materials across to unity. I played around with real time lighting and emission based lighting for a more realistic look but found it was too much of a load for my computer. I switched the emission lights out for regular lighting and some pre baked lighting. ​
